David Beckam Biography

David Robert Joseph Beckham

Former Professional Footballer & Global Ambassador

David Robert Joseph Beckham, OBE (born 2 May 1975) is an English retired professional footballer who played as a midfielder for Manchester United, Real Madrid, LA Galaxy, and Paris Saint-Germain. Born and raised in Manchester, Beckham represented England at the 1996 Summer Olympics and was part of the national team that finished as runners-up in the 2002 and 2006 FIFA World Cups.
